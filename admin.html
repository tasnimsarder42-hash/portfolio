Manage your website data visually.

How to update your website:
  1. Edit your LinkedIn posts or Behance projects below.
  2. Click Save & Download at the top right.
  3. The browser will download a file named data.js.
  4. Move this new data.js file into your AntiGravity Ai Soft folder, replacing the old one!
  5. Push the changes to GitHub.
